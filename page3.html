Legion Technologies Internship

This was my first experience working in a tech company, especially in a startup setting with senior professionals. Legion Technologies is a company that specializes in workforce labor optimization. It's main product is building an app that allows hourly workers to optimize the hours of their shifts and makes it much easier for them to schedule their shifts. environments.

My main job was working with their error logs. I went through all the system error logs in Grafana Loki and wrote many queries to identify their top 10 exceptions. Below in an image of an example query I wrote in Grafana Loki and the chart generated.

Finally, I was tasked with finding an alternative error handling and logging system to their current one that they used. I researched and implemented Rollbar for error tracking to replace current error tracking conducted in Grafana Loki. I found out that this system was a much better alternative than their current one for it was much faster and much more cost-effective.
