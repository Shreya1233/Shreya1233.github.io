SciQuel Web Development Internship

This internship was my first real experience with web development. My first main project was to design one of the web pages for the website, which is how I became acquainted with HTML and CSS. I worked with 30 individuals from many different universities across the world. Communication and Teamwork skills were tested much more greatly due to the huge time zone differences, and I learned how to maintain constant communication via Slack or even impromptu Zoom calls.

I enhanced and developed the interface of SciQuel's app with React, facilitating the dissemination of scientific articles. I also pitched and developed UI design ideas in biweekly meetings.

This experience taught me the importance of effective communication and teamwork, especially in a remote working environment.
